About Us

Our Story and Values

Our Story

Welcome to Lassi Corner, where we've been serving the community since 2010. What started as a small lassi shop has grown into a beloved gathering place for lassi, shakes, and food lovers alike.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing the highest quality lassi and food while creating a warm, welcoming environment for our customers. Our passion for excellence drives everything we do.

Our Lassi Variety

From classic sweet and salted lassi to exciting fruit blends like mango, strawberry, and banana, we offer something for every taste. Try our special dry fruit, rose, and seasonal flavors for a refreshing twist!

Our Food

All our pastries and sandwiches are made fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. We believe in supporting local farmers and producers while serving delicious, high-quality food.
